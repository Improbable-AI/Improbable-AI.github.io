Logo Design:
Our logo is a stylized, open triangle with rounded corners and a dot, representing the letter "A" for "AI" and evoking a sense of motion, creativity, and approachability. The open form symbolizes openness to new ideas and collaboration, while the dot adds a playful, human touch.

Color Scheme:
The gradient transitions from vibrant pink (#ff5ecb) to electric blue (#5ecbff), reflecting the lab's energy, innovation, and forward-thinking spirit. These colors are used throughout our branding to create a modern, dynamic, and inclusive visual identity.
